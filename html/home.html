More About Us

Many of our members have been attending Arise for Jesus Ministry for years,
but we’ve got a growing group of newcomers and are enthusiastic about welcoming more.

Our History

On February 28, 2011 Arise for Jesus Ministry relocated
to EGoli 1 in Amsterdam Zuidoost,where there worship up to date.
Arise for Jesus Ministry is a multinational and non-denominational
ministry organisation in Europe.

Our Vision And Mission

Our vision is to bring people before God’s presence in prayer, worship
, praise and preach the word of God that will change their lives and
create an atmosphere of love

What We Believe In

Having been led as we believe by the spirit of God, to receive the lord Jesus
Christ as our saviour, and on profession of our faith having been baptised in the
name of the father and of the son and of the Holy Spirit.
